Cultural maternity/paternity leave

Cultural maternity/paternity leave is is a project initiated by active parents of the city of Kyiv, who wanted to lead a normal lifestyle even after the birth of a child - visiting cinemas, museums or restaurants. Therefore, they suggested that museums try to organize tours for parents with small children. Our museum also joined this project with joy!
